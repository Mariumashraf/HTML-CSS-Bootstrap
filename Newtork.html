Network Fundementals

the best references and tutorials to study Computer Networks

    Books:
  • 1."Computer Networking: A Top-Down Approach" by James Kurose and Keith Ross

    Book Review:
    The book teaches the complex subject of computer networking through a layered approach in a "top-down manner."
    The book covers important concepts that help your learning of the study of networking.
    This one of the best Computer Network book also focuses on the Internet and the important issues of networking.
    It offers an excellent foundation for readers who want to deep dive into the world of computer science without going in deep into programming or mathematics.

  • 2."Data Communications and Networking" by FOROUZAN

    Book Review:
    The book presents this complex technical subject matter without relying on
    complex formulas with the help of 700 figures given in the book.
    This is one of the best computer networking book that presents the fundamentals of knowledge about this subject in a clear, straightforward manner.
    The "bottom-up" approach helps instructors to cover the material in one course.
